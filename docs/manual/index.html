BirdWatchAI User Manual

Everything you need to identify birds at your feeder — automatically.
Windows Desktop App Raspberry Pi Server Docker Community at www.birdwatchai.com

BirdWatchAI watches a camera pointed at your bird feeder and uses AI to identify the species that visit — saving photos, recording short clips, and sending you a notification when something interesting shows up. This manual covers both editions: the original Windows desktop app and the newer cross-platform server (Raspberry Pi, Docker on Windows, or any Linux box).

New here? Start with these three.

1. Read Part 1 — Introduction to understand what BirdWatchAI does and which edition fits your setup.

2. Follow Part 2 — Installation for your platform (Windows, Pi, or Docker).

3. Walk through Part 3 — First-Time Setup to point the app at your camera.
